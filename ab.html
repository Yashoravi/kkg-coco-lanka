ABOUT KKG COCO LANKA

Premium Sri Lankan Coconut Export Company

KKG Coco Lanka is a Sri Lanka-based manufacturer and exporter specializing in high-quality coconut-based products including coco peat, coir fiber, grow bags, coconut oil, natural food products, and spices for international markets.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in sustainable agriculture by pioneering innovative coco peat solutions while promoting eco-friendly farming and sustainable Sri Lankan coconut resources worldwide.

Our Mission

Deliver premium sustainable coconut products with exceptional quality, modern production technology, environmentally responsible practices, and customer-focused export solutions.
